Health


The Harvard Medical School offers some
advice for avoiding injuries when playing golf:


Stretch at least three times a week.
Warm-up for 10-15 minutes before you play.
Consider taking lessons to improve your technique.
Use good equipment, including clothing and gloves.
Don’t ignore early strain signs.
Use ice on aching tissues after playing and use the PRICE (Protection, Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation) approach to treat more serious problems.
When in doubt, always seek the advice of your medical practitioner.
